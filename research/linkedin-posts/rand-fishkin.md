Search happens everywhere. Not just Google, ChatGPT, and Bing. Our 
latest project with Datos, A Semrush Company analyzed search on 41 
major websites via their desktop panel and found that if you broaden 
the definition of search, Google is 73%, not 90%+. And ChatGPT is 
3.2%, well behind several players rarely mentioned in the conversation.

And, if billions of searches are taking place on dozens of sites, 
not just "traditional" and "AI tools," maybe SEO should be bigger 
than those two categories as well ¯\_(ツ)_/¯
