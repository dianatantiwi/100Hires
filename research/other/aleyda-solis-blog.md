## Why This is Relevant
Aleyda provides a practical framework and audit checklist for 
what makes brands consistently show up in AI search — directly 
actionable for AI-powered SEO content production strategies.

## Key Takeaways
- Brands that win in AI search share 10 key characteristics: 
  Accessible, Useful, Recognizable, Extractable, Consistent, 
  Corroborated, Credible, Differentiated, Fresh, and Transactable
- Content must be structured so AI can easily extract and 
  summarize it — not just readable by humans
- Corroboration across third-party sources is critical — AI 
  systems trust brands mentioned across multiple platforms
- Freshness matters — stale content gets cited significantly 
  less than updated content
- The checklist can be used as a practical AI search audit 
  tool for any brand's content strategy
