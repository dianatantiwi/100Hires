## Why This is Relevant
Dixon Jones reviews his own tool Waikay — showing exactly how 
AI systems understand and represent brands, and what content 
production strategies are needed to fix gaps and 
misinformation in AI answers.

## Key Takeaways
- AI systems don't just rank pages — they build associations 
  between brands and topics that content must actively shape
- Waikay reveals three critical questions: how often you show 
  up (Brand Visibility), what AI believes about you (Topic 
  Reports), and which sources underpin those beliefs 
  (Source Tracker)
- AI hallucinations about brands are common — content 
  producers must actively monitor and correct what AI 
  systems say about their brand
- GEO Action Plans convert AI visibility findings into a 
  concrete content production backlog
- Being mentioned across many sources matters more than 
  ranking first in one — volume and distribution of 
  content beats depth in a single channel
- AEO and GEO are not interchangeable — AEO is about being 
  the confidence anchor for a binary fact while GEO is 
  about being the authority AI trusts to explain a synthesis
