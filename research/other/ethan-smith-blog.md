## Key Takeaways
- ChatGPT, Perplexity, Gemini and other chat surfaces now use 
  search to power answers — AEO and SEO are converging
- AEO topics are clusters of questions that a single page 
  targets — rather than single keywords
- Most SEO and AEO work is wasted — focus on the 5% of 
  strategies that drive outsized impact
- Adding statistics, quotations, and citations to content 
  boosts AI visibility by up to 40%
- LLM traffic converts 6x better than Google search traffic 
  because users arrive with much higher intent
- Early-stage startups can win at AEO immediately unlike SEO 
  which requires years of domain authority
