## Why This is Relevant
Koray shares deep insights on how search engines are evolving 
under LLM-driven retrieval systems and what it means for 
content production — directly relevant to AI-powered SEO 
content strategy.

## Key Takeaways
- Search engines evolving under LLM-driven retrieval systems 
  fundamentally changes how content must be structured and 
  produced
- Topical Authority and Semantic Content Networks remain the 
  foundation of visibility in both traditional and AI search
- AI Overviews and query fan-out dramatically change click 
  models — content must be optimized for retrieval not just 
  ranking
- Cost of Retrieval is the defining metric of the future — 
  the easier it is for AI to retrieve your content, the 
  higher its value
- Building brand-driven demand alongside technical SEO 
  fundamentals is essential for surviving the AI search era
- Underlying principles of information retrieval remain 
  constant even as SERP features and algorithms change
