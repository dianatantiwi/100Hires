## Why This is Relevant
Lily Ray exposes a critical problem in AI-powered SEO content 
production — AI systems are recycling misinformation at scale, 
creating a dangerous feedback loop that threatens content quality 
and search integrity.

## Key Takeaways
- AI systems treat repetition as consensus — if enough sources 
  say something, it becomes "fact" regardless of accuracy
- Lily published a fake core update on her blog and within 24 
  hours AI Overviews, Gemini, and ChatGPT were serving it as fact
- Over 2,000 false articles are currently in circulation about 
  a Google update that never happened
- AI-generated content at scale is poisoning the information 
  ecosystem that LLMs train and retrieve from
- Content producers must prioritize accuracy and originality 
  over volume to avoid contributing to the AI Slop Loop
