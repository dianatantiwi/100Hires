## Why This is Relevant
Marie Haynes documents how Google's AI systems have fundamentally 
changed what content needs to look like to rank and get cited — 
essential reading for anyone producing AI-powered SEO content.

## Key Takeaways
- Google's AI ranking systems prioritize user satisfaction over 
  vector optimization — content must genuinely help people
- The agentic web is replacing traditional browsing — websites 
  must be structured for AI agents to read and act on them
- The 3 C's of AI Readiness: content must be Clear, Complete, 
  and Consistent for AI agents to trust and cite it
- MCP, A2A and UCP protocols are the new technical SEO — 
  websites must implement these to be discoverable by AI agents
- TurboQuant reduces Google's vector search indexing time to 
  virtually zero — meaning fresh content gets evaluated faster 
  than ever
- E-E-A-T signals are more important than ever as AI systems 
  evaluate content trustworthiness before citing it
