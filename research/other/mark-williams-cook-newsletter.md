## Why This is Relevant
Mark's Core Updates newsletter covers the most important weekly 
developments in AI search and SEO — this Google I/O special 
edition directly covers how AI Mode and AI Overviews are 
changing content production requirements.

## Key Takeaways
- CTR drops by 61% when AI Overviews are present and 41% 
  even when they are not — content must now optimize for 
  visibility inside AI answers not just click-through
- 97% of URLs linked in AI Mode appear in the top 10 organic 
  results — traditional SEO still powers AI search citations
- Google is integrating AI Mode into the main search tab — 
  AI-powered answers are becoming the default search experience
- LLM traffic delivers lower conversion rates than paid and 
  organic channels — volume of AI citations matters more 
  than individual clicks
- Non-commodity, first-hand experience content consistently 
  outperforms generic AI-generated content in both traditional 
  and AI search results
