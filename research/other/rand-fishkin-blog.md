## Why This is Relevant
Rand's original research redefines where SEO content needs to 
be produced and distributed — showing that AI-powered SEO 
content production must go beyond Google to win visibility 
across all search surfaces.

## Key Takeaways
- Search happens everywhere — not just Google, Bing, and 
  ChatGPT but across 41 major websites including YouTube, 
  Reddit, Amazon, and Instagram
- Google still dominates with 73.7% of search but ChatGPT 
  only holds 3.2% — far behind Reddit, Amazon, and YouTube
- SEO should mean Search Everywhere Optimization — content 
  must be produced for all platforms where audiences search
- AI content production strategy must account for Reddit, 
  YouTube, and review sites as these heavily influence 
  what LLMs cite
- The companies that built multi-platform content presence 
  years ago are now winning AI citations at scale
